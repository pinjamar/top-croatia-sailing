About us

We specialize in offering yacht charter services with an experienced crew. You will be accommodated with the best boat selection for your charter preference with our fleet of more than 300 boats. Our base is in Croatia and we offer an all-inclusive sailing experience around beautiful islands Brac, Hvar, Vis, etc. The focus is very much on the details, from the offer to make your trip more enjoyable to the authentic feel of the boats themselves. We are available for any questions and/or sudden change of heart with whatever issue you may have – we will gladly facilitate it all. It'a family business of two cousins trained to offer any type of assistance and is very much committed to our clients, partners and boat owners. Choose among our many extras such as sup, sup with led lights for nighttime, go pro videos as a lifetime memory, icebox, grill, etc. and let us know your preferences via email or phone. If you wish your trip to be completely stress-free you can also entrust us with the transfer - all you have to do is provide the crucial details such as your flight number, etc. and enjoy the smooth ride while we take care of everything else.
